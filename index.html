Time has been flying by. it seems like just yesterday i dropped to my knees when i saw those two pink lines!

Funny how now getting kicked is just another thing to me.

less than 12 weeks until we get to meet her! it seems like so far away but i know it will come quick. im definitely excited to have my body back but i'll miss the peace knowing that she's safe and protected!
